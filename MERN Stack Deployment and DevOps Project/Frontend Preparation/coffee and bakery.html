Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew & Bake started as a small family-owned coffee stand with a passion for quality and community. What began as a humble operation has grown into a beloved neighborhood destination.

We source our coffee beans directly from sustainable farms and our bakery uses only the finest ingredients, with many items made from scratch daily.

Our mission is simple: to create a welcoming space where people can enjoy exceptional coffee and baked goods while connecting with others.
